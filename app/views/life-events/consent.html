Agree to share your child Disability Living Allowance information

To automatically apply for free school transport, you need to agree to share your Disability Living Allowance information with Arnsford District Council.

Arnsford District Council will use your:

  • Child's name
  • Child's date of birth
  • Child's school
  • Child's disability affects they're disability
  • Name
  • Postal address
  • Email address
  • Phone number

Read the terms of use and privacy policy for Arnsford District Council to check how your information will be used.

If you do not agree to re-use information from your child Disability Living Allowance, you will need to fill out the application form.
