Department for Work and Pensions

Child Disability Living Allowance

Get notifications to your public service when users successfully apply for child Disability Living Allowance.

Who might use this notification

This life event notification is most likely to be of use to public services designed to support families of children with disabilities.


Data included in these notifcations

These life event notifications can include:

  • Child's name
  • Child's date of birth
  • Child's school
  • Child's post address
  • How a child's disability affects their disability
  • Parent or guardan's name
  • Parent or guardan's email address
  • Parent or guardan's phone number

Available as


What data can be use for

DWP has agreements with public sector organisations to use data for the following purposes:

  • Transport and mobility services for children with disabilities
  • Financial support to families of children with disiabilities
  • Housing services for families of children with disabilities

View all data sharing agreements

The agreements do not let you use the data for any other reason.

For data to be used for new purpose, completely new agreements would need to be made, which is a longer process on average of five months.

If you're unsure whether the agreements fits what you want to do, contact the GDS Data Sharing Legal team: legal-data@digital.cabinet-office.gov.uk

Request to get these notifications

Only local authorities, NHS trusts and government departments can request to get these notifications.

You'll need to provide a reason why you need access to a new life event data. If the request is approved by your department Data Protection Officer, it can take several months to get access. It's not guaranteed.
